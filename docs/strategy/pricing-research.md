# CaliLean Competitive Pricing Research

**Date:** 2026-04-30
**Scope:** 8 competitors analyzed, 30 launch SKU variants priced

---

## Executive Summary

Comprehensive pricing analysis across 8 peptide/compound competitors reveals CaliLean's original pricing was significantly misaligned with market reality. GLP compounds (Semaglutide, Tirzepatide, Retatrutide) were priced 2-4x above market rates, while core peptides (BPC-157, TB-500) had placeholder pricing ($1-$3). Updated pricing positions CaliLean in the **competitive mid-market** tier, undercutting premium vendors while maintaining margins above budget competitors.

## Key Market Findings

### Price Leaders (Lowest)
- **Ion Peptide** dominates on price with flat pricing ($29-$49 for most peptides regardless of size)
- **Purgo Labs** competitive on BPC-157 ($24.99/5mg) and GLP compounds
- **Crush Research** aggressive on bulk (5-pack bundles at 30% off)

### Mid-Market
- **Core Peptides & Biotech Peptides** - established brands, higher prices, larger catalogs
- **Flawless Compounds** - premium positioning with rewards program, spray/capsule forms

### Premium Tier
- **Mile High Compounds** - 7x tested, cGMP certified, account-required model
- **Verified Peptides** - smaller catalog, up to 15% volume discounts

### Volume Discount Models
| Competitor | Model | Structure |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Ion Peptide | Per-product tiers | 10% (4-5), 16% (6-9), 25% (10+) |
| Crush Research | Bundle pricing | 5-packs at ~30% off |
| Mile High | Site-wide promos | Up to 20% off select, 10% new accounts |
| Flawless | Loyalty points | Earn/redeem points per purchase |

## CaliLean Pricing Strategy

**Position:** Competitive mid-market with volume incentives
**Goal:** Undercut Core/Biotech/Mile High while maintaining quality perception above Ion/Purgo

## Proposed Volume Discount Structure

Modeled after Ion Peptide (market leader in volume incentives):

| Tier | Quantity | Discount | Example: BPC-157 10mg |
|------|----------|----------|-----------------------|
| Base | 1-3 | 0% | $59.99 |
| Stack | 4-5 | 10% off | $53.99 |
| Save | 6-9 | 15% off | $50.99 |
| Bulk | 10+ | 20% off | $47.99 |

At 10+ units with 20% off, CaliLean's effective prices approach Ion Peptide's base pricing while maintaining higher perceived value.

## Competitive Advantages to Emphasize

1. **Transparent pricing** - No account required to see prices (unlike MHC, Flawless, Crush)
2. **Large GLP variant sizes** - 20mg and 30mg sizes that most competitors don't offer
3. **Stack and Save** - Clear, per-product volume discounts visible on product page
4. **Unique blends** - GLOW (70mg) and KLOW (80mg) proprietary formulations
5. **Wolverine stack** - Pre-blended BPC/TB at value pricing vs buying separately
