# `cl` monogram — deferred to v2

The CMO brief ([identity-brief.md §6](../../identity-brief.md)) defers the monogram. v0 is wordmark-only.

A `cl` ligature monogram will be developed in a v2 pass for use on:
- Vial caps (foil-stamped or laser-etched)
- Loyalty merch (hat, tee, water bottle)
- Favicon / app icon (16/32/48px)
- Clinician letterhead

This folder is a parking spot. Do not ship anything from here without sign-off. Two early sketches sit here as conversation starters for the v2 conversation, not as decisions.

## Sketch ledger

- `sketch-horizon.svg` — sun-on-horizon mark. Coastal/local-first signal. Calm, embroiders well. Does not encode `cl`.
- `sketch-framed-cl.svg` — `CL` set in a soft-rounded apothecary frame. Encodes the letters; reads as a lab seal. May be useful for COA badges even at v0 if needed (separate ask).

## v2 brief sketch (for whoever picks this up)

- Encode `cl` (lowercase preferred, to match the wordmark casing).
- Same single-weight philosophy as the wordmark.
- Single-stroke or single-fill so it embroiders and laser-etches without rasterization tricks.
- Must hold its identity at 16×16px (favicon).
- Must not become a "molecule," "leaf," "drop," "shield," or "hex" mark per brief §6 anti-patterns.
