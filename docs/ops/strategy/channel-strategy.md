## 0. The frame

**Strategic:** RUO posture. Most ad platforms classify peptides as restricted or prohibited. **We were never going to win on paid acquisition.** We win on transparency, geography, and the founding-customer flywheel.

**Tactical:** South Bay is the proof market. Every channel choice in the first 90 days makes the local-first signal louder.

**Channel priorities (rank order):**

1. **Owned: storefront + email + lab book.** The most defensible.
2. **IRL: South Bay venues + run/tri/surf clubs + the Strand pop-up.**
3. **Earned: press + organic creator seeding.**
4. **Organic social: IG + TikTok.** Founder-led + UGC. Slow build.
5. **Paid: narrowly geo-fenced, narrowly worded.** Last on the list. Max $2k/wk in window 1.

## 2. Organic social — IG + TikTok

### 2.1 Principle

We are not trying to be loud. We are trying to be **the account a researcher saves**. Depth, restraint, and a single visual language.

### 2.2 Format mix

| Format | Use | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| **A — COA / batch carousel** | The signature post. Mass-spec trace, batch number, overlaid in **JetBrains Mono**. | 2x/week |
| **B — Strand still-life** | Vial on concrete, on tile, on clipboard. Pre-dawn. No people. | 2x/week |
| **C — Founder talking-head (60-90s)** | CEO or advisor explains one compound's primary literature. No claims. | 1x/week |
| **D — UGC repost** | Lab book on coffee table, vial in home gym. Permission + attribution. | 1-3x/week |
| **E — Education-hub teaser** | 1-2 frames from a long-form essay; CTA: "read the full citation." | 1x/week |

**What we don't do:**
- Trends, influencer dance overlays, before/after photos.
- Emoji in feed captions.
- Therapeutic claims, dosing, medical advice. **A post that crosses this gets pulled same day.**
- **Generic drug names for CL- series compounds in any social content.**

### 2.3 Cadence

| Channel | Posts/week (window 1) | Stories/week |
|---|---|---|
| **Instagram** | 5 (Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri/Sat) | 8-12 |
| **TikTok** | 3 (Tue/Thu/Sat) | n/a |
| **Threads / Bluesky** | Off in window 1. | — |

## 4. Email program

Five flows live by day 0. A sixth layers in by day 30.

| # | Flow | Trigger | Length |
|---|---|---|---|
| F1 | Welcome (waitlist) | Card-QR signup | 3 emails / 14 days |
| F2 | Founding-100 launch | Slot redeemed | 4 emails / 30 days |
| F3 | Education series | List entry | 6 emails / 60 days |
| F4 | Restock / batch announcement | New COA published | 1 email per lot |
| F5 | Win-back | No purchase 90+ days | 2 emails |
| F6 | Post-purchase education (per-SKU) | Order placed | 3 emails / 30 days |

### 4.1 Voice & ops rules

- **From-name:** "CaliLean" for transactional, "Joe / CaliLean" for founder-voice.
- **Subject lines:** lowercase, short (6 words max). No exclamation points. No emoji.
- **No discount codes in window 1.**
- **Plain-text variant ships on every email.**
- **ESP:** Klaviyo. Domain auth + DMARC strict before day 0.
- **CL- series:** email copy uses CL- codes and receptor descriptions, never generic drug names.
