## How to Use This File

When you make a technical, architectural, or strategic decision during a work session, add an entry here. This prevents future sessions from re-debating settled questions or unknowingly reversing a deliberate choice.

**When to log:** If you or an agent spent more than a few minutes evaluating options before choosing an approach, log it.

### Entry Format

```markdown
### ADR-NNN: {{Title}} ({{YYYY-MM-DD}})

**Status:** {{Accepted | Superseded by ADR-NNN | Deprecated}}
**Decided by:** {{Name(s)}}
**Context:** {{What problem or question prompted this decision?}}
**Decision:** {{What was decided?}}
**Alternatives considered:**
- {{Option A}} — {{Why rejected}}
- {{Option B}} — {{Why rejected}}
**Consequences:** {{What does this decision mean going forward?}}
```
